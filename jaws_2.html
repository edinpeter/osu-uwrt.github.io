Jaws 2

Weight 15 lbs Controller Intel NUC / ArbotiX-M
Dimensions 23.5" x 14.5" x 9.0" Thrusters 3x CrustCrawler 400HFS-L
Cameras 3x Point Grey Blackfly
Servos Dynamixel MX-28T IMU Microstrain 3DM-GX4-25

The 2014-2015 ROV based on its predecessor, Jaws, was appropriately named Jaws 2. The actuating thruster mechanism is very similar to Jaws, but the rear thruster has been moved to allow more forward thrust, as well as easier access to the electronics vie the rear endcap. The forward camera dome was machined in-house on a CNC mill to have a perfect hemisphere tip with parabolic sides. This gives the operator an undistorted video feed and the Jaws 2 less resistance traveling through water.

Features:
  • 2x Actuating Thrusters
  • Stereo Vision System
  • Machined Aluminum Electronics Mount on Stationary Slide Shafts
  • 5.25" Diameter x 14" Long Main Housing
  • Pneumatic Claw (4.5" Long)
  • Parabolically Milled Arcylic Camera Dome (0.5" Thick)
