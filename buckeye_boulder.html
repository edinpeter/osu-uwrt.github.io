Buckeye Boulder

Weight 18 lbs Controller None
Dimensions 30.0" x 25.0" x 17.0" Thrusters 8x Bilge Pump Motors
Cameras None

The Buckeye Boulder was the Underwater Robotic's Team first attempt at a remotely operated vehicle. Consisting of a PVC frame painted scarlet and gray, this vehicle was covered with school spirit. Unfortunately, the small budget and lack of electronics limited the capabilities of the vehicle. Thus earning its name, the Buckeye Boulder.
