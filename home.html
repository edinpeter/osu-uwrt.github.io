2014-2015 Season is Underway!

The UWRT is hard at work, coming together as groups 3 times a week, designing the next batch of robots that includes both a Remotely Operated Vehicle (ROV) and an Autonomous Unmanned Vehicle (AUV). We welcome our newest members who have shown great ambition and willingness to learn. We are still actively accepting members though our main recruitment efforts have come to a close. If you are a current student at The Ohio State University and find yourself interested in joining this fantastic team, feel free to contact us with your inquries.

New Website

We have just implemented a new website format for the UWRT. We are always striving to follow a path of excellence so we will continue to update and better the website. If you have any suggestions or reccomendations, again feel free to contact us.
