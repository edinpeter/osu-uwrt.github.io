STEMbot

Weight 3 lbs Controller Raspberry Pi
Dimensions 11.0" x 10.0" x 3.5" Thrusters 3x Brushless Hobby Motors
Cameras Raspberry Pi Camera Module

The STEMbot is a small ROV developed for STEM outreach activities. It is comprised of a 3D-printed printed housing, three brushless thrusters (two forward, one vertical), and a forward-facing camera. With a Raspberry Pi controller, electronic speed controllers, and a camera on-board, the STEMbot is far superior to other small ROV kits. Normally kits use PVC as the ROV structure, pool noodles for buoyancy, and a simple on-off control system. We hope to bring ROV education to the next level, allowing students to program the controls, design their own 3-D printed housings, and learn about waterproofing techniques. With a simple to use camera that connects directly to the Raspberry Pi, students can venture into the exciting underwater abyss.

Features:
  • 3D Printed housing
  • Forward-Facing Camera
  • On-Board Electronics
  • Variable Speed Control
